Privacy Policy for Post Fix Pro

Effective date: March 27, 2026

Post Fix Pro is a calculator app. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Post Fix Pro does not require you to create an account, and we do not intentionally collect or store your calculator entries on our own servers.

The app may use third-party services that process limited device or app data in order to provide advertising and in-app purchases.

Advertising

Post Fix Pro uses Google AdMob to display ads in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect or process information such as device identifiers, IP address, diagnostics, app interactions, and advertising data in accordance with Google's policies.

Learn more about how Google uses data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

In-App Purchases

Post Fix Pro uses Google Play Billing to offer a one-time in-app purchase that removes ads. Payment transactions are processed by Google Play, not by us. We do not receive or store your full payment card information.

How the App Uses Data

  • To display ads in the free version of the app
  • To process and restore the ad-removal purchase through Google Play
  • To maintain basic app functionality and diagnostics

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data processed through AdMob or Google Play Billing is handled by those service providers under their own policies.

Children's Privacy

Post Fix Pro is not directed to children under 13. If your Play Console audience settings ever change to include children, this policy and the app's configuration should be reviewed before publishing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
